**Thematic Organization:**

**Theme 1: The Dual-Scope Architecture**
_Focus: Clearly separating Private vs. Community spaces._
- **Nested Prayer Scopes:** Strict boundary between Personal (User-owned) and Church (Tenant-owned) data.

**Theme 2: The Guidance-Based UI**
_Focus: Moving from passive display to active direction._
